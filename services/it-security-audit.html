IT Security Audit

We specialize in the following area.
  • Application Penetration Testing
  • Mobile Application Assessment
  • Source Code Review
  • Architecture Security Assessment
  • Product Security Review

    • In today's cloud computing infrastructure, your application is your weakest link. Bad guys constantly attacks your application to steal user's data and try to get access to your network. Without a secure application, your organization will be vulnerable to a number of threats. Sodality will work with you to secure your infrastructure. We provide a comprehensive security auditing services based on your comfort level. We can work with your developer and audit source code line by line to make sure they are fully secure. If you are not comfortable with sharing your source code, that's perfectly fine. We can do a blackbox penetration testing on your web and mobile application. We will show you a proof-of-concept on how the attackers can break your application. We can also help you secure your application from architecture perspective. Our experts will perform a thorough threat modeling to identify your weak area. We will review your integration with 3rd party services to make sure that the integration doesn't introduce any hole to your application.

    We take our work very seriously. We enjoy working with our clients and we are confident that our great service make them feel the same. If you don't want to let the bad guys break your application, please contact us. We will break and fix your application before they do.
